Chain Maps

Maps on blockchain?

ChainMaps is a simple web geocaching system, decentralized and based on Ethereum blockchain. Its purpose is to make maps impossible to censor by third parties. It’s perfect for hacktivists, independent organizations or users who don't want to depend or give their data to corporations.

Example map

How it works

The app is executed in a web browser from chainmaps.net or locally downloading a (Zip) version. Works in html/js without backend.

Online version is executed against infura.io node, and downloadable versión can be configured to be used against our own local ethereum node. This avoid any posibility of censorship.

Contact: chain.maps[at]yandex.com

Test network
